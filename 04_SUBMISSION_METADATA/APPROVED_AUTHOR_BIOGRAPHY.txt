Yilmaz Vural is a Professor in the College of Technology and Engineering at Westcliff University. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Hacettepe University and completed postdoctoral research in Computer Science at the University of California, Santa Barbara. His research focuses on cybersecurity, privacy-preserving computing, data anonymization, IoT security, network resilience, and security governance. He has more than 25 years of experience across academia, government, defense, and industry, including leadership roles in data protection and secure digital infrastructure. He also serves as an expert evaluator for the Eureka/Eurostars Programme.
